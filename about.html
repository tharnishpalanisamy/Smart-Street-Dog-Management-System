About Us

Learn about our street dog management initiative

Our Mission

We are dedicated to managing street dog populations through humane, scientific methods that prioritize both animal welfare and public safety. Our comprehensive approach combines modern technology with proven veterinary practices to create sustainable solutions for communities across India.

How It Works

  • Humane Capture: Dogs are captured using safe, stress-free methods by trained professionals
  • Medical Care: Each dog receives comprehensive health assessment and necessary treatments
  • Animal Birth Control (ABC): Sterilization prevents future population growth
  • Vaccination: Dogs receive rabies vaccination and other essential immunizations
  • Unique Identification: Each dog is fitted with a collar containing a unique digital ID
  • Database Entry: All information is recorded in our comprehensive digital database
  • Territory Return: Dogs are returned to their original territory after full recovery

Our Approach

Our digital collar system represents a breakthrough in street dog management. Each collar contains:

  • Unique identification number (District-Area-DogID format)
  • QR code for quick information access
  • Durable, weather-resistant design
  • Comfortable fit that doesn't restrict natural behavior

Impact & Results

Since our program began, we have achieved significant improvements in:

  • Reduced dog bite incidents in managed areas
  • Improved vaccination coverage against rabies
  • Better coordination between community members and authorities
  • Enhanced public awareness about humane animal treatment
