About Us

Learn about our street dog management initiative

Our Mission

We are dedicated to managing street dog populations through humane, scientific methods that prioritize both animal welfare and public safety.

How It Works

  • Dogs are captured humanely for Animal Birth Control (ABC)
  • Each dog receives vaccination and sterilization
  • Dogs are fitted with unique ID collars
  • Information is recorded in our digital database
  • Dogs are returned to their territory

Contact Information

For emergencies or urgent reports, please contact our helpline.

About Rabies

Important information about rabies prevention and treatment

What is Rabies?

Rabies is a viral disease that affects the nervous system of mammals, including humans. It is almost always fatal once symptoms appear.

Prevention

  • Vaccinate your pets regularly
  • Avoid contact with stray animals
  • Report aggressive or sick animals
  • Seek immediate medical attention after any animal bite

What to Do After a Dog Bite

  1. Wash the wound thoroughly with soap and water
  2. Apply antiseptic
  3. Seek immediate medical attention
  4. Report the incident to local authorities
