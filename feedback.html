Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We strive to respond to all feedback within 48 hours. Complex issues may take up to 5 business days for a comprehensive response.

Can I submit feedback anonymously?

Yes! You can choose to submit feedback anonymously. However, providing contact information helps us follow up and provide better assistance.

What happens to my feedback?

All feedback is reviewed by our management team. Suggestions are evaluated for implementation, complaints are investigated, and compliments are shared with relevant staff.

How do you use the feedback for improvement?

Feedback is analyzed monthly to identify trends and areas for improvement. This data directly influences our training programs, service modifications, and strategic decisions.
